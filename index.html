Projects

Predictive Power Outage Modelling

Developed a machine learning model to predict severe weather-related power outages in the Greater Sydney region, used to optimse the reliability of residential battery systems. Model was trained on historical weather and outage data that required extensive pre-processing such as geospatial mapping and feature engineering to ensure accurate data representation. A Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network was implemented to detect long-range dependencies in weather patterns. Model acheived high accuracy but limited recall in outage prediction, highlighted key challeng in predicting anomalies such as grid outages in networks.
